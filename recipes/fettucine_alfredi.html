Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Description

Pasta that is cooked in bechamale and has a delicious cheesy base and contributes chicken as well as a light dash of spices.

Begin by preparing the sauce in a saucepan. Add milk and heavy cream and reduce it to a healthy consistency. To this add cooked chicken to your preference and add a dash of salt and basil leaves. Now begin preparing pasta in separate pan. Use the shape of your choice and cook to your preference. Add the sauce once a little amount of water remains in the pan.

Ingredients :D

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Cheese
  • Milk
