Biriyani Recipe

Description

A delicious rice-based meal that has slow-cooked mutton and a variety of spices that culminate to form an explosion of flavor and luxury.

Begin by stirring a large amount of onions to form a flavorful base. These onions are to be chopped into manageable sizes and will be cookes in oil or butter. After this you may add a variety of spices. Add some water to allow all the flavors to merge and reduce. Let this happen for a few minutes after which we will add mutton and then rice.

Ingredients :D

  • Rice
  • Mutton
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Butter
